Zombies

"Ugh... that smell can only mean zombies."―Alyx Vance

Ordinary Zombies are often referred to as Standard Zombies to distinguish them from the other types of Zombies, such as the Gonome, the Fast Zombie, the Poison Zombie, the Zombine. As their name suggests, Zombies do not appear to retain much of their former intelligence and, like Headcrabs, will blindly pursue their prey, oblivious to danger (such as traps). Zombies do not respond to fire, and will walk blindly into it, though they do feel it on them and notice they are burning once they are on fire. Zombies mainly attack with swipes from their claws. It is not currently known if a person is technically alive after turning into a Zombie. However, the groaning that standard Zombies constantly make are heard as a victims' muffled attempts to yell for help. This seems to indicate that a victim is likely alive and at least somewhat aware of their situation. And, indeed, ripping a headcrab off of a victim shows that a victim's head is bent upwards at an irregular angle, with a terrified look and a violent scream to match frozen onto their face. A Headcrab's attack does little damage (with the exception of Poison Headcrabs), and it also takes some time for Headcrabs to take over their hosts, who are initially still in control; however, Headcrabs both attack very swiftly and (should they succeed) latch onto the head very firmly, so that any attempt by a struggling victim to remove the parasite with their hands is largely futile. Coupled with disorientation and the imminent loss of consciousness from suffocation, this ensures that most victims nonetheless succumb very quickly. It is not known for sure if a victim of a Headcrab attack can still be rescued once a Headcrab has attached itself to their head. Given the massive modifications the Headcrab makes to its victim - particularly the opening up of the ribcage - even if removal could be achieved, death would likely be near-instantaneous regardless. Headcrabs can be removed shortly after they latch onto a person's face, as Headcrabs can be killed and removed before they do any damage.
