Tau Cannon (Gauss Gun)

The Tau Cannon, also known as the XVL1456 and/or Gauss Gun, is a powerful particle accelerator. The Tau Cannon can be first found in black mesa Questionable Ethics, on the second floor of the Advanced Biological Research Lab. If not obtained there, it can be found in the Lambda Reactor Core supply depot at the end of the chapter Lambda Core. A Gauss Gun (or coilgun) is a device that uses electromagnetism to accelerate projectiles, and has some similarity to a railgun. The weapon uses the same ammunition as the gluon gun, which is obviously an energy weapon, giving further evidence to the weapon not being a true Gauss Gun. Though the early Tau Cannon prototypes used normal household batteries as an initial power source for the particle accelerator, a micro-fission reactor generated the deadly particle beam. When the Tau Cannon alternate fire mode was used the reactor would be fed more depleted Uranium-235 fuel without being fired, and the energy would be stored in a set of spinning capacitors, which will cause a catastrophic discharge if it is held for too long.
