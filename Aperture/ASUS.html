Aperture Science Unstationary Scaffold™

"Good. Now use the Aperture Science Unstationary Scaffold to reach the chamberlock." ―GLaDOS[src]

The Aperture Science Unstationary Scaffold is a testing element featured in several Test Chambers of the Enrichment Center.
